The Flash: The Return of Barry Allen

By Mark Waid and Matt Hollingsworth

5$

Book description

A resurrected Barry Allen surprises Wally West by appearing at his door on Christmas. The two try working together, but trouble arises over sharing the identity of The Flash. The returned Barry Allen is more violent than before, which leads Jay Garrick to recruit two old speedsters to stop him: Johnny Quick and Max Mercury. Meanwhile, the discovery of a biography of Barry leads Wally to figure out who the new Barry really is: Eobard Thawne, the Reverse-Flash.
