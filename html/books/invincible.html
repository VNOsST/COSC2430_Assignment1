Invincible

By Robert Kirkman

5$

Book description

Four months into the future, a flying teenage superhero known as Invincible (Mark Grayson) has arrived in Antarctica, carrying a man who has a bomb strapped to his chest. In the present, Deborah Grayson is trying to convince her son Mark to get out of the bathroom so he won't be late for school. Mark finishes reading his Science Dog comic book in there and walks into the kitchen to get breakfast. His mom turns on the TV, where they see the superhero known as Omni-Man fighting a dragon in Taiwan. Mark casually comments how Omni-Man is his dad. Later that day, Mark has to reject his friend William's invitation to hang out, as he has work. At work, while attempting to toss a trash bag into the dumpster, Mark accidentally sends it flying through the sky, indicating that he now has Superhuman Strength.
