Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information:

    2. When you make a purchase or create an account with the Bookstore, we collect certain personal information, such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment information.

  2. Device Information:

    3. We automatically collect certain information about your device, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system. This information helps us optimize and improve our website and ensure its security.

  3. Usage Information:

    4. We collect information about your interactions with the Bookstore, such as the pages you visit, the products you view, and the search terms you enter. This information helps us understand your preferences and provide a personalized experience.

Use of Information

1. We use the personal information we collect to:

  • Fulfill orders
  • Process payments
  • Provide customer support

2. We may use your personal information to:

  • Communicate with you about your orders
  • Send you important updates or promotional offers
  • Respond to your inquiries

3. We may use device information and usage information to:

  • Analyze trends
  • Monitor the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Improve our website's functionality and performance

Sharing of Information

1. We may share your personal information with third-party service providers that assist us in performing certain functions necessary to operate the Bookstore, such as payment processing, order fulfillment, and website hosting.

2. We may also share your personal information to:

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to legal requests
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Investigate fraudulent or illegal activities

3. In the event of a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

1. You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, update, or delete your personal information. You may do so by accessing your account settings or contacting us directly.
  • Unsubscribe from our marketing communications at any time by following the instructions provided in the emails we send or by contacting us.

Please note that we may need to retain certain information for record-keeping purposes or as required by law.

Children's Privacy

The Bookstore is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as possible.

The Bookstore's website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites or services. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised version will be effective as of the "Last Updated" date mentioned below. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.
